About

Album covers, if designed well, have the power to give us insight into the music on an album, attract buyers, and stand as art in themselves. Because jazz typically lacks lyrics the cover is especially important in understanding the mood, tone, and style of the music. Here you will find insight into the design of jazz covers from the late 1940s to the early 1960s, as well as information on the designers themselves.
